Personal Overview


My interests in science and engineering began from an early age. I'm fascinated by both theory and application, so there's practically no limit to what I enjoy learning.
I can typically be found up late printing parts with my Prusa MK4S 3D Printer, experimenting with high-voltage transformers, assembling stepper motor control circuits, or researching new material science concepts.
My driving philosophy is 'science for the sake of science,' and I believe the ability to understand complex problems is our most valuable trait.


Outside of engineering, I've enjoyed playing the guitar, drums, and keyboard for most of my life. I'm also a big fan of history and philosophy, which I believe both positively reinforce STEM learning.


I'm always interested in professional opportunities and connecting with like-minded individuals. If you'd like to work together please reach out!
